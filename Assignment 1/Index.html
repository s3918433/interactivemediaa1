Interactive research of https://gehry.getty.edu/ by RESN TEAM

"What was the first thing you paid attention to when interacting with the experience?"

The scribbled animated abstract lines that go across the webpage caught my attention, as they are the first thing you'll see when viewing the page. It makes you wait for it to finish drawing, curious about what they are scribbling and what they are making. It will then lead you to the introduction page, where it automatically animated the big word ‘Sculpting Harmony', which is designed for users to be ‘attracted’ to it. That way, it leads them to hover their mouse over or even click on it as they want to know ‘what does it do’. Otherwise, the stretch animation makes you want to scroll down and see what’s to come.

''Spend two minutes with the experience and create a list of each of your discrete actions.''

  • Clicked anywhere in the screen; starts playing songs and the menu pops up
  • Hover my mouse across ‘Sculpturing Harmony’ as i was mesmerized by their text effects
  • Scrolled down; leads to a drone-like background of the venue; keep scrolling back and forth as it was satisfying to play with.
  • Scrolled down after playing with the background, leads to the video documentary
  • Stopped watching it halfway because I noticed the ‘Check out model’ text and wondered what that led me to
  • Link leads me to the architecture’s prototype, which was 3D, played with it for a while
  • Scrolled down when i got bored; scanned through the paragraph section
  • Scrolled down more; leads to the next chapter
  • 2 minutes ends here
